# 🏥 Healthcare Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive healthcare analytics dashboard built in Tableau. It analyzes patient demographics, billing patterns, and admission trends to provide a clear, data-driven view of healthcare operations.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify trends in patient volume, cost drivers, and demographic distribution, supporting informed decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze patient admission trends over time 
- Evaluate average billing amounts across medical conditions 
- Understand patient demographic distribution (gender) 
- Provide high-level KPIs for operational insights 

---

## 📌 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Patients:** 55,500 
- **Average Billing Amount:** $25,539 
- **Average Patient Age:** 51.54 years 

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1. Patient Admissions Over Time (Line Chart)
- Tracks monthly patient admissions segmented by medical condition 
- Identifies seasonal trends and fluctuations in patient volume 

### 2. Billing by Medical Condition (Bar Chart)
- Displays average billing amounts across conditions:
  - Cancer 
  - Diabetes 
  - Hypertension 
  - Obesity 

### 3. Patient Distribution by Gender (Pie Chart)
- Illustrates the proportion of male vs. female patients 
- Provides insight into demographic balance 

---

## 🔍 Trend Analysis

### 📅 Patient Admissions Trends
- Patient admissions remain relatively **stable throughout the year** 
- Minor fluctuations suggest normal operational variation 
- Slight increases mid-year may indicate seasonal demand 
- No extreme spikes or drops → indicates **consistent patient flow** 

---

### 💰 Cost Analysis (Billing by Condition)
- **Cancer treatments show the highest average billing amounts** 
- Chronic conditions (Diabetes, Hypertension) show steady, moderate costs 
- Revenue is driven by:
  - High-cost treatments (major contributors) 
  - Chronic conditions (consistent baseline revenue) 

---

### 👥 Patient Demographics
- Gender distribution is **nearly equal (~50/50 male and female)** 
- Indicates balanced access and utilization of healthcare services 
- No significant demographic disparity observed 

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- The organization demonstrates **operational stability** with consistent patient admissions 
- Revenue is influenced heavily by **high-cost medical conditions** 
- Chronic conditions provide a **reliable and recurring revenue stream** 
- Balanced gender distribution suggests **equitable service access** 

---

## 🏁 Conclusion
Based on the data presented, the healthcare organization appears to be operating in a **stable and efficient state**.

- Patient volume remains consistent, indicating steady demand 
- Billing patterns reflect a mix of high-cost and chronic condition treatments 
- Demographic distribution shows balanced engagement across genders 

Overall, the organization demonstrates:
- **Strong operational consistency** 
- **Sustainable financial performance** 
- **Balanced patient demographics** 

This dashboard highlights opportunities for continued optimization, particularly in managing high-cost treatments and monitoring subtle seasonal trends in admissions.
